Why Sayble 为什么选 Sayble

Most voice typing tools charge monthly fees, lock you into their speech engine, and route your voice through their servers. Sayble is different. 市面上的语音输入工具按月收费，绑定引擎，语音数据全部经过他们的服务器。Sayble 不一样。

You choose the engine 引擎自选

Plug in any ASR provider or OpenAI-compatible LLM you like. ASR 和润色 LLM 都由你决定，接入任何兼容 API 即可。

Pay API costs only 按量付费

No subscriptions, no tiers, typically a few cents per day. 没有会员、没有套餐，直接用官方 API 原价，一个月可能只花几块钱。

You own your data 数据自主

Everything stays local, no accounts, no telemetry. 所有数据留在本地，无账号、无遥测。

Features 功能

Global hotkey 全局热键

Press a key in any app to start voice typing, works system-wide. 在任意应用中按下快捷键即可录音，全系统可用。

Real-time streaming 实时流式识别

Words appear as you speak, powered by WebSocket-based ASR. 边说边出字，基于 WebSocket 流式 ASR。

LLM polish LLM 润色

Optionally refine raw speech into clean text — fix typos, casual-to-formal, etc. 可选接入大模型，自动修正错别字、口语转书面语。

Persona system 人设系统

Switch between custom prompts for different writing styles. 自定义润色 Prompt，一键切换不同写作风格。

Floating indicator 浮窗提示

A small overlay shows live transcription and recording timer. 录音时悬浮窗实时展示识别文字和计时。

System tray 系统托盘

Runs in the background, out of your way. 常驻后台，不占任务栏。
